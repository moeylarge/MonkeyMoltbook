## Shot-by-shot assembly notes

## SHOT 1 — Central Peak master

### Use
- open the sequence
- light cafe ambience starts here
- optional soft music bed begins under the room

### Motion
- extremely light push-in only
- no flashy zoom

### Hold length
~5 seconds

### Notes
This is where the world breathes.
Do not rush it just because it is an establishing shot.

---

## SHOT 2 — Couples Night sign

### Use
- hard cut from Shot 1
- let the sign read quickly and clearly

### Motion
- optional micro push-in

### Hold length
~4 seconds total including line and reaction space

### Dialogue
**Jojo:** “Couples Night?”

### Notes
Leave a small beat after the line so the audience processes the premise.

---

## SHOT 3 — Group notices sign

### Use
- hold long enough for multiple reactions
- this is the ensemble ignition point

### Motion
- mostly static
- if any motion, keep it minimal and centered

### Hold length
~6 seconds

### Dialogue
**Fufu:** “No, no — let’s not pretend this isn’t for us.”

### Reaction notes
- Mon visibly tightens
- Chance enjoys this immediately
- Russ starts collapsing internally
- Rachelle becomes curious
- Jojo looks around sincerely

### Notes
Do not crowd this with too much extra dialogue.
Faces matter here.

## SHOT 4 — Mon + Chance

### Use
- first real romantic-banter pressure point

### Motion
- very light push-in recommended

### Hold length
~9 seconds

### Dialogue
**Chance:** “You look tense for someone who’s definitely not part of a couple.”

Do not edit this beat too tightly.

## Audio assembly notes

### Required layers
1. cafe ambience under full scene
2. dialogue track
3. optional light score bed

### Optional
- tiny lift/sting entering pair beats
- small release after final line

### Avoid
- melodrama
- trailer score
- fake sitcom laugh cues

---

## Motion rules

### Allowed
- subtle push-ins
- very light hold-based motion
- simple cuts

### Avoid
- heavy zooms
- kinetic transitions
- parallax gimmicks
- overproduced motion graphics

---

## If the cut feels thin

Adjust in this order:
1. add more reaction hold
2. soften line pacing
3. reduce overlap clutter
4. increase ambience
5. only then consider adding one new regroup shot

Do not immediately generate more assets.
