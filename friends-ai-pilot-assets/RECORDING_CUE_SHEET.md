# NOT A COUPLE — RECORDING CUE SHEET

## Purpose

This file is the direct temp-voice recording guide for the **64-second v2 animatic**.

Use it to record each line with:
- character
- exact wording
- target timing
- performance intention
- pause guidance

This is not the final dub script.
This is the first practical VO sheet for assembly.

---

## Recording rule

Record for **natural performance**, not perfect polish.

Priority:
1. believable delivery
2. emotional timing
3. room energy
4. clarity
5. polish last

If a line sounds too “written,” do another take with less performance and more reality.

## Stop rule

Once you have one usable take per cue, stop.

Do not chase perfection through 30 takes.
The point of temp VO is to make the cut real enough to judge.
