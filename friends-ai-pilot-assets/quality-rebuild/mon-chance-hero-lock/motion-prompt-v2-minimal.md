# Mon + Chance Motion Prompt v2 Minimal

## Use rule
Only use this after a still candidate passes the still gate.

## Prompt
Animate this approved Mon and Chance still into an ultra-restrained premium episodic shot inside Central Peak.

Preserve character identity exactly from the source still. Preserve the exact composition, spacing, eyelines, wardrobe, hand positions, background continuity, and emotional power balance.

This is a near-still performance beat. Use only tiny believable living motion:
- subtle breathing
- one soft blink at most
- microscopic eye movement
- a barely perceptible head or posture settle
- slight natural finger stillness / cup hold realism only if it remains stable

Keep Mon controlled, guarded, narrow-eyed, and mildly affected. She must not soften, smile, warm up, or become cute.
Keep Chance dry, amused, and quietly provocative without changing facial family.

Do not add camera motion. Do not push in. Do not reframe. Do not change distance between them. Do not invent gesture arcs. Do not create a new expression beat. The goal is to preserve the still while adding only enough life to read as filmed rather than frozen.

Faces must remain stable and attractive throughout. Lighting must stay warm and coherent. Motion must feel natural, expensive, and almost invisible.

## Negative constraints
- no face morphing
- no identity drift
- no expression drift
- no smile growth
- no Mon softening
- no rubber motion
- no twitching
- no exaggerated gestures
- no floating shoulders
- no hand drift
- no cup drift
- no extra limbs
- no chaos wobble
- no camera move
- no dramatic camera move
- no push-in
- no posture rewrite

## Runtime target
Aim for the shortest clip that can still feel alive: approximately 5 seconds.

## Review target
Reject immediately if the motion makes the still worse.
