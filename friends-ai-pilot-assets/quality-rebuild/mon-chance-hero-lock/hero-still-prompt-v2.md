# Mon + Chance Hero Still Prompt v2

## Goal
Create a premium AI-cinema episodic still that can serve as the visual style anchor for the entire `Not a Couple` rebuild.

## Prompt
Create a premium AI-cinema medium two-shot inside Central Peak, a warm comfort-sitcom cafe. Focus only on Mon and Chance. Keep the composition intimate, directed, and emotionally specific, like a finished frame from a premium animated romantic-comedy series rather than concept art or promo art.

Mon should read controlled, sharp, guarded, and emotionally affected while trying not to show it. Chance should read dry, amused, subtly provocative, and socially comfortable without looking dominant or smug. Their relationship tension must be legible at a glance. This should feel like awkward romantic pressure under normal cafe conversation, not a fashion pose and not generic attractive-couple energy.

Use a clean medium two-shot with deliberate spacing, readable depth, warm cinematic cafe lighting, natural eye lines, polished facial finish, and stable anatomy. Keep the Central Peak world believable and cozy in the background. The shot must feel premium, authored, and episode-ready.

## Negative constraints
- no mascot or fursuit look
- no cartoon softness
- no over-smiling
- no fashion campaign or poster pose
- no glamour ad energy
- no exaggerated flirting
- no extra characters
- no duplicate heads or limbs
- no warped hands
- no flat background blur that kills the cafe world
- no melodramatic camera angle

## Framing notes
- medium two-shot
- eye-line tension matters more than body pose
- intimate but not melodramatic
- directed scene frame, not key art

## Review target
Reject if the image looks merely pretty.
Accept only if it clearly communicates Mon/Chance tension before dialogue.
