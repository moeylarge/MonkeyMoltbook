# NOT A COUPLE — EXPANDED PILOT BEATS

## Purpose

This file expands the current 7-shot teaser board into a **60–90 second pilot structure**.

The goal is to turn the proof-of-concept opener into a real short-form episode sequence.

This is still not the final full 4-minute pilot.
It is the first proper episode-length structure that can actually breathe.

---

## Recommended runtime target

**Primary target:** 60–90 seconds

Why:
- long enough to feel like a real episode scene
- short enough to finish without exploding scope
- ideal for testing tone, chemistry, and pacing

---

## Episode premise

At Central Peak, the group notices a **Couples Night** promo.
That immediately destabilizes the room because everyone becomes weird about who is or is not a couple.

The point is not the sign itself.
The point is how the sign exposes the group dynamic.

---

## Structure overview

### ACT 1 — Trigger
1. establish Central Peak
2. reveal Couples Night sign
3. group notices sign

### ACT 2 — Pair pressure
4. Mon + Chance tension
5. Russ + Rachelle awkwardness
6. Jojo + Fufu chaos

### ACT 3 — Group spiral
7. everyone starts assigning meaning
8. social discomfort spreads
9. Chance escalates with the “discount” button

## Expansion rule

Do not jump to dozens of new shots.

Expand in this order:
1. use the current 7 shots
2. stretch timing with dialogue and reactions
3. if the sequence still feels thin, add exactly **one** regroup shot
4. only after that consider inserts
