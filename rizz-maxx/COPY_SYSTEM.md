# COPY_SYSTEM.md

## Voice
Confident, sharp, modern, direct.

## Must feel
- premium
- useful
- slightly bold
- emotionally charged without sounding trashy

## Avoid
- fake hype
- cringe flirt language
- pseudo-scientific certainty
- robotic over-explanation
- spammy masculinity clichés

## Core framing
Talk about:
- profile strength
- first impression
- lead-photo quality
- set quality
- weak-photo drag
- improvement potential

Do not talk like:
- guaranteed match outcomes
- universal beauty truth
- laboratory-grade certainty

## Example UI copy direction

### Onboarding
- `Fix the photos that are costing you matches.`
- `Find your strongest lead photo. Remove the ones dragging your profile down.`

### Upload
- `Upload 4-10 photos`
- `The more honest the set, the better the optimization.`

### Analysis loading
- `Finding your strongest first impression...`
- `Flagging the photos that hurt the set...`

### Results summary
- `You have a few strong assets, but your current set is leaking value.`
- `Your lead photo has potential. The rest of the set needs cleanup.`
- `You’re closer than your current profile makes it look.`

### Action plan tone
- direct
- concise
- practical

Examples:
- `Replace your current lead with a cleaner, sharper shot.`
- `Cut the blurry mirror selfie. It lowers the whole set.`
- `Add one well-lit full-body photo to balance the profile.`

### Premium framing
- `Unlock the full profile strategy.`
- `See the exact order, deeper photo breakdowns, and what to replace next.`
