## App Store description

**Stop guessing which photos are helping your dating profile — and which ones are quietly hurting it.**

RizzMaxx helps you improve your dating profile by analyzing your full photo set, identifying your strongest lead photo, flagging weak images, and showing you exactly what to fix next.

Instead of rating one selfie in isolation, RizzMaxx looks at your profile as a full first impression.

### What you can do with RizzMaxx
- upload a real dating-photo set
- see your overall profile strength
- find your best lead photo
- identify the first photo to remove
- compare old vs new sets
- track profile upgrades over time

### Why it works
A dating profile is not just one good picture.
It is the full set:
- the first impression your lead photo creates
- the consistency of the lineup
- the weak photos that drag the profile down
- the changes that make the whole set stronger

RizzMaxx is built to help you improve that full picture.

### Premium unlocks deeper strategy
With Pro, you can unlock:
- deeper breakdowns
- smarter photo ordering
- replacement guidance
- stronger lineup strategy
- saved progress across profile versions

### Built for practical improvement
RizzMaxx is designed to feel useful, direct, and honest.
No fake guarantees. No cringe pickup-coach spam. Just clearer feedback, stronger profile decisions, and faster iteration.

If you want a better first impression, start with the photos.
