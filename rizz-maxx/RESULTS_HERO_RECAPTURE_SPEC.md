## What the current screenshot likely got wrong
The previous `03-results.png` underperformed because too much of the visible frame emphasized system/meta framing instead of user payoff.

The recapture should feel less like:
- app internals
- platform state
- generic result UI

and more like:

- immediate action
- obvious value

## Visual pass/fail test
The screenshot passes only if, within about 2 seconds, a new viewer can tell:
- there is a score
- there is a strongest photo
- there is a weakest photo
- the app is helping them improve the full profile

If any one of those is unclear, recapture again.

---

## Capture instructions
- use a visually strong sample set
- make sure the best photo and weakest photo are visually distinct
- choose a result state where the score and summary feel meaningful
- crop to minimize unimportant lower-page sections
- do not let the screenshot look like a generic dashboard

---

## Final composition recommendation
### Preferred frame
- top: full `ResultsHero`
- middle: most of `Best photo to lead with`
- bottom: enough of `First photo to remove` to clearly read the section and image

### Composition rule
If the full first-cut section cannot fit cleanly, recrop until the section title and image are still clearly visible.
Do not sacrifice the best/weakest proof just to show more generic surrounding UI.
