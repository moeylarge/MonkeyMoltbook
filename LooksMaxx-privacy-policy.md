# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 23, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 23, 2026

LooksMaxx ("LooksMaxx," "we," "us," or "our") provides a mobile app for face-photo analysis, score interpretation, archetype and category breakdowns, improvement guidance, compare/battle features, and progress tracking (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use LooksMaxx.

By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information we collect:
- directly from you
- automatically through your use of the Services
- from photos, device interactions, and app activity needed to provide the Services

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, platforms, or services that have their own privacy policies and terms.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide Directly
We may collect information you provide to us, including:
- photos you upload or capture for analysis
- support requests, feedback, and communications
- any optional information you include when contacting us

### B. Analysis and App-Generated Data
To provide the LooksMaxx experience, we may generate and process:
- face-analysis outputs
- scores, potential ranges, archetype interpretations, and category breakdowns
- improvement-plan suggestions
- battle/compare results
- progress and scan-history records
- device-local export records created from your scans

### C. Device, Usage, and Log Information
We may automatically collect technical and usage information, including:
- app version
- device type and operating system
- crash or error information
- timestamps and feature usage
- diagnostic logs needed to troubleshoot reliability or abuse

### D. Local Device Storage
LooksMaxx stores certain scan history, export records, and app state locally on your device so the app can function properly and show progress over time.

## 3. How We Use Information

We may use information we collect to:
- provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Services
- analyze submitted photos and generate results
- save local history and progress views
- support compare/battle and sharing features
- troubleshoot bugs, monitor performance, and improve reliability
- respond to support requests and user communications
- detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, misuse, or security issues
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies

## 4. How We Share Information

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

### A. Service Providers
We may share information with vendors or service providers that help us operate the Services, such as hosting, infrastructure, analytics, communications, storage, or security providers.

### B. Legal Compliance and Protection
We may disclose information if we believe disclosure is necessary to:
- comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
- enforce our agreements or policies
- protect the rights, property, or safety of LooksMaxx, our users, or others
- detect, prevent, or investigate fraud, abuse, misuse, or security incidents

### C. Business Transfers
If LooksMaxx is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, asset sale, reorganization, or similar transaction, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to:
- provide the Services
- maintain app functionality and requested features
- comply with legal obligations
- resolve disputes
- enforce agreements
- support security and operational needs

Information stored locally on your device may remain there until you delete the app, clear app data, or otherwise remove it.

## 6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:
- access certain information we hold about you
- request correction of inaccurate information
- request deletion of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below. We may need to verify your identity before responding.
