# LooksMaxx

LooksMaxx gives you a sharper read on how your current presentation lands — and where your biggest gains are hiding.

Upload a photo to get:
- a looks read
- score and potential
- archetype interpretation
- category breakdowns
- improvement guidance
- compare / battle mode
- progress tracking

See what is helping your score, what is holding it back, and what changes are most likely to improve the result.

## Why LooksMaxx

LooksMaxx is designed to feel clear, fast, and actionable.

Instead of vague feedback, it aims to show:
- where your current read is landing
- which categories are helping most
- what is suppressing the result
- where your highest-upside improvements are
- how your presentation changes over time

## Launch note

LooksMaxx is launching free first so early users can test the product, give feedback, and help improve the experience during the launch window.
