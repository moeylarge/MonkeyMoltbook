# FRIENDS AI
## DRIFT REVIEW FRAMEWORK

Use this after each generated asset so we don’t guess emotionally or regenerate randomly.

Rule:
score, diagnose, then regenerate only the broken layer.

---

## 1) Review scale
- 5 = locked
- 4 = usable with minor polish
- 3 = borderline
- 2 = weak
- 1 = reject

---

## 2) Master review rule
For each asset, score:
1. silhouette / form
2. face / expression
3. wardrobe / styling
4. tone fit
5. world fit
6. originality / non-copy feel
7. usefulness for production continuity

Then give:
- overall score
- keep / revise / reject
- what exactly drifted
- what layer to tighten

## 4) Character-specific review rules

### Mon
Must feel: elegant, controlled, refined, slightly intense, premium
Common drift: too cold, too soft/cute, too bridal, too editorial and not sitcom-readable

### Russ
Must feel: sweet, awkward, thoughtful, sincere, lovable
Common drift: too goofy, too creepy, too handsome and smooth, too cartoon giraffe

### Jojo
Must feel: warm, charming, easy, social, lovable
Common drift: too dumb-looking, too influencer-bro, too athletic/frat, too generic dog-man

### Chance
Must feel: sleek, dry, attractive, observant, guarded
Common drift: too villainous, too smug, too fashion-model, too loud/flashy

### Fufu
Must feel: expressive, memorable, theatrical, funny, emotionally intuitive
Common drift: too random, too childish, too costume-y, too loud visually

### Rachelle
Must feel: glamorous, polished, socially magnetic, warm underneath, romantic
Common drift: too shallow/model-only, too pageant, too gaudy, generic glam with no soul

### Gunty
Must feel: grounded, familiar, dry, practical
Common drift: too quirky, too stylized, too comedic-looking, too visually loud

---

## 5) Group lineup review
Score:
- silhouette separation
- color separation
- cast cohesion
- lead/support readability
- tonal unity
- premium feel
- thumbnail readability

Questions:
- can each character be identified instantly?
- does the cast feel like one show?
- does Gunty clearly read as support?
- does the cast feel premium and warm rather than furry/cartoon/kids-content?

Rule:
if 2+ characters feel wrong, do not move to set generation yet.

## 7) Set-specific review rules

### Central Peak
Must feel: iconic, warm, couch-centered, socially inviting, stylish, returnable
Common drift: too close to Central Perk, too generic coffee shop, too trendy/sterile, too dark/moody

### Apartment 2A
Must feel: curated, elegant, polished, emotionally intimate, Mon structure + Rachelle glamour
Common drift: too sterile, too showroom, too feminine-chaotic, not enough warmth

### Apartment 2B

### Hallway

---

## 8) Drift diagnosis categories
- silhouette drift
- styling drift
- tone drift
- emotional drift
- copy drift

Fix the weakest layer first instead of rewriting everything.

---

## 9) Keep / revise / reject rules
- KEEP when overall score is 4.5+
- REVISE when overall score is 3 to 4.4
- REJECT when overall score is under 3

---

## 10) Move-forward gate
Do not move to pilot masters until:
- all 7 character looks are at least 4/5
- at least 4 characters feel truly locked
- Central Peak is locked
- 2A is locked
- 2B is locked
- hallway is usable
- cast and world feel like the same show
- nothing feels cheap or too derivative

---

## Final rule
If an image is only pretty, that is not enough.
We are locking usable canon, not attractive drift.
