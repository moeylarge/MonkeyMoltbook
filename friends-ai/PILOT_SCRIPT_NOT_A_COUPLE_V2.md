# FRIENDS AI
## Pilot Episode 1 — Not a Couple
### Script v2
### Length target: ~4 minutes

---

## SCENE 1 — COLD OPEN
### INT. CENTRAL PEAK — DAY

Warm café energy. Stylish, cozy, familiar.
The group occupies the main couch/table zone like they’ve unofficially claimed it forever.

MON has a perfectly arranged drink.
RACHELLE looks polished without trying.
CHANCE is sprawled like rules are optional.
JOJO is mid-snack.
RUSS is perched awkwardly, never fully settled.
FUFU bursts in with immediate emotional weather.

Behind the counter, GUNTY dries a cup and watches the group with expert detachment.

A chalkboard reads:
TONIGHT — COUPLES NIGHT
2 FOR 1 DRINKS

FUFU
Oh, no.

JOJO
That’s usually your exciting voice.

FUFU
It is. I’m excitingly concerned.

MON
About discounted beverages?

FUFU
About what discounted beverages reveal.

CHANCE
Nothing stable has ever followed that sentence.

Fufu points dramatically at the sign.

FUFU
By tonight, this group will know too much.

RACHELLE
About coffee?

FUFU
About each other.

Russ accidentally looks at Rachelle. She catches it instantly.

RACHELLE
Why did Russ just look at me like I’m the final question on an exam?

RUSS
I didn’t.

CHANCE
You absolutely did.

JOJO
Honestly, this could be fun. We come back tonight, pair up, save money, everybody wins.

MON
Pair up as what.

A beat.

Fufu smiles like a prosecutor with evidence.

FUFU
We already have couples.

MON
No, we do not.

FUFU
Emotional couples count.

GUNTY
In here, they usually count more.

FUFU
Thank you, Gunty.

GUNTY
That was not support.

CUT TO TITLE.

---

## SCENE 2
### INT. CENTRAL PEAK — CONTINUOUS

JOJO
Okay, simple. We split into pairs, act natural, get cheaper drinks.

MON
There is no version of this group that acts natural.

CHANCE
That may be the healthiest thing you’ve ever said.

FUFU
Fine. I’ll assign.

MON
You will not assign.

FUFU
Mon and Chance.

MON
No.

FUFU
Immediate. Defensive. Beautiful.

CHANCE
I do appreciate being chosen first.

MON
No one chose you. You were inflicted.

JOJO
That was flirty.

MON
That was not flirty.

CHANCE
It had rhythm.

Rachelle turns to Russ, enjoying herself.

RACHELLE
What about you?

RUSS
What about me.

RACHELLE
Who would you hypothetically be not-dating for reduced-price drinks?

RUSS
That is an insane sentence.

CHANCE
And yet somehow your worst nightmare.

JOJO
You could just say Rachelle.

RUSS
I was not going to just say— why would I just—

FUFU
Oh, he’s cooking. Badly.

Fufu stands and points around the group like she’s mapping a crime board.

FUFU
Official emotional pairings: Mon and Chance — tension. Russ and Rachelle — panic in formalwear. Me and Jojo — joy, danger, upper-body movement—

JOJO
I receive that.

MON
There are no pairings.

FUFU
Then why are all of you sitting like custody was already decided?

They all look down.
Mon and Chance are close.
Russ is angled entirely toward Rachelle.
Jojo and Fufu are somehow shoulder-to-shoulder already.

GUNTY
Would now be a bad time to mention you all ordered in pairs.

JOJO
Did we?

GUNTY
You physically formed them.

---

## SCENE 3
### INT. APARTMENT 2A — LATER

A beautiful apartment. Polished, styled, intentional.
Mon enters with tightly managed irritation.
Rachelle drops onto the couch, removing her shoes with practiced elegance.

Fufu is already there, upside down in a chair like she materialized.

MON
That entire conversation was ridiculous.

FUFU
You said that in the elevator, the hallway, and before opening the door. So whatever this is, it’s organized now.

MON
I am always organized.

FUFU
Not like this. This is romantic organization.

RACHELLE
Yeah. This is your “I’m fine but every pillow needs to face north” mood.

Mon starts fixing a pillow that was already fine.

MON
I am not in a mood.

FUFU
You’re fluffing furniture over a man.

MON
I am fluffing because that pillow was wrong.

RACHELLE
And was Chance also wrong.

Mon stops.

MON
Chance is... irritating.

FUFU
That was tender.

MON
That was not tender.

FUFU
No, but it had seasoning.

RACHELLE
He does get under your feathers.

MON
You are not allowed to phrase things like that.

RACHELLE
I’m sorry, would you prefer “spiritually disruptive”?

FUFU
You only get this controlled when you like somebody.

MON
That is not true.

FUFU
Mon. You alphabetized your tea.

Mon glances at the neatly aligned tea shelf.

MON
That was preexisting.

RACHELLE
Sure. And I was born glamorous.

---

## SCENE 4
### INT. APARTMENT 2B — SAME TIME

Warm, casual, more chaotic.
Chance leans against the kitchen counter.
Jojo is on the couch with chips.
Russ paces like he’s trying to outrun his own brain.

RUSS
She said, “Who would you hypothetically be not-dating for reduced-price drinks?”

JOJO
That’s flirtation.

CHANCE
That’s not automatically flirtation.

JOJO
No, but it’s definitely flirt-adjacent.

RUSS
What does that mean.

JOJO
It means if language wore cologne, that sentence did.

CHANCE
Every day with you is educational against my will.

RUSS
Do you think she meant something?

CHANCE
Yes.

RUSS
What.

CHANCE
That watching you panic is a recreational activity.

Russ deflates.

JOJO
No, that can still be good.

RUSS
How.

JOJO
Because if she didn’t like you at all, she wouldn’t poke you. She’d just let you sit there and be tall.

RUSS
That’s actually... not bad.

CHANCE
Don’t encourage his hope. It gains speed.

RUSS
Because maybe the joke was the safe version of the real question, which means socially she has plausible deniability, but emotionally—

CHANCE
I’m leaving before this gets footnotes.

JOJO
See? Even he knows it was flirty.

---

## SCENE 5
### INT. HALLWAY — NIGHT

Apartment doors open almost at once.

Mon exits 2A.
Chance exits 2B.
They nearly collide.

MON
You’re blocking the hallway.

CHANCE
You say that like I’m not improving it.

Across the hall, Jojo drags Russ out by the sleeve.
Rachelle steps out behind Mon.
Fufu emerges last, delighted by the geometry of the moment.

FUFU
Wow. This hallway has chemistry.

MON
No, it has poor timing.

CHANCE
Same thing, sometimes.

They all head toward the stairs / exit together.

---

## SCENE 6
### INT. CENTRAL PEAK — NIGHT

Couples Night. Slightly fuller crowd. Warmer lighting.
The six settle in without discussing it.

And immediately, despite themselves:
Mon sits beside Chance.
Russ beside Rachelle.
Jojo beside Fufu.

Gunty approaches, pad in hand.
He looks at the arrangement. Then at them.

GUNTY
Great. Three couples.

MON / CHANCE / RUSS / RACHELLE / JOJO / FUFU
No. / What? / We’re not— / Excuse me? / I mean— / Yes, exactly—

Everyone turns to Fufu.

FUFU
What? I’m tired of lying.

Gunty waits.

GUNTY
So... six full-price drinks.

A painful beat.

Chance glances at Mon.

CHANCE
I’m willing to claim Mon for financial reasons.

Mon slowly turns to him.

MON
I’m sorry?

CHANCE
Strictly economic.

JOJO
That is the hottest cheap thing I’ve ever heard.

RUSS
Why would you say that out loud?

RACHELLE
No, let him. This is excellent.

FUFU
I need tonight to last forever.

Mon stares at Chance.

MON
You think this is funny?

CHANCE
A little.

Mon tries not to smile. That’s the real button.

SMASH CUT TO BLACK.
