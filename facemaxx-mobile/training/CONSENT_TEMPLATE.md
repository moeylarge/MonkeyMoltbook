# LooksMaxxing Consent Template

Use this when collecting real participant photos.

## Short consent message

I agree that my submitted photos may be used internally for LooksMaxxing testing, analysis, labeling, and model development.

## Slightly fuller version

I agree that the photos I submit may be used internally for LooksMaxxing testing, face-analysis evaluation, dataset labeling, and future model development. I understand this is for internal product development and not public posting.

## Suggested record fields
- Participant ID
- Date
- Consent text used
- Name (optional)
- Contact (optional)
- Consent given via (text / email / in-person / form)
