# LooksMaxxing Rating Rubric

## Purpose
Human raters should label faces consistently enough to train a v1 scoring model.

## Core rule
Rate the visible presentation in the provided image, not the imagined person outside the image.

## Scale
Use 0-100 for each score.

## Required ratings
- overall attractiveness
- jawline
- eyes
- skin
- symmetry
- hair / framing
- facial harmony

## Category guidance
### Overall attractiveness
Overall first-impression facial appeal in this specific image.

### Jawline
How strong, clean, and well-defined the lower face reads.

### Eyes
How striking, clear, and well-positioned the eye area appears.

### Skin
Texture, clarity, tone, and apparent skin health.

### Symmetry
How balanced and aligned the face appears in this image.

### Hair / framing
How well hair, hairline, and face framing support the face.

### Facial harmony
How well the features work together as a whole.

## Additional labels
- archetype guess
- image quality
- confidence in your rating
- notes on angle / lighting / occlusion

## Rating warnings
Do NOT over-interpret if:
- angle is extreme
- image is blurry
- lighting is poor
- multiple faces are present
- face is partially blocked

## Consensus policy
- minimum 3 raters per face
- average scores for training labels
- store disagreement spread
- flag low-consensus images for review
