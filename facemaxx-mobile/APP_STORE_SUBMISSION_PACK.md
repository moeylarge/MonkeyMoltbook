### Description
LooksMaxx gives you a sharper read on how your current presentation lands — and where your biggest gains are hiding.

Upload a photo to get:
- a looks read
- score and potential
- archetype interpretation
- category breakdowns
- improvement guidance
- compare / battle mode
- progress tracking

See what is helping your score, what is holding it back, and what changes are most likely to improve the result.
