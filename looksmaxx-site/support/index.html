If you need help with LooksMaxx, have a bug report, want to request data deletion, or want to report a problem with a scan or upload flow, contact us at:

Email: moeylarge@gmail.com

Best way to contact support

When possible, include:

  • your device model
  • iOS version
  • app version
  • a short description of what happened
  • screenshots if relevant
  • whether the issue happened during upload, camera capture, scan, breakdown, battle, history, or share

Common support topics

1. Upload or camera issues

If photo upload or camera capture is not working:

  • make sure the app has camera and photo-library permission
  • try a clearer, front-facing image
  • avoid heavily compressed screenshots when possible
  • try again with better lighting and a more centered face

2. Scan quality issues

If a result feels weak or provisional:

  • use a cleaner photo
  • avoid blurry or dark images
  • keep the full face visible
  • avoid extreme angles

3. Progress/history questions

LooksMaxx may save scan history and progress data locally on your device so you can review prior scans and compare results over time.

4. Privacy and deletion requests

For privacy, deletion, or data-related requests, email moeylarge@gmail.com.

Response time

We aim to respond as quickly as practical, but response times may vary depending on request volume.
