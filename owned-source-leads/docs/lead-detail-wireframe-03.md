# Lead Detail Wireframe 03

## Operator-first stacked layout

### Section 1 — Decision summary
- hot/warm/cold/junk
- score
- export readiness
- review action buttons

### Section 2 — Source proof
- exact source
- page or source URL
- attribution trail
- timestamp

### Section 3 — Full collected data
- raw field view
- normalized field view if applicable

### Section 4 — Why this lead matters
- why scored that way
- why marked hot
- fit summary
- risk notes

### Section 5 — Consent and compliance
- inbound consent fields
- disclosure version
- export block warnings

### Section 6 — History
- duplicate checks
- review actions
- export actions
