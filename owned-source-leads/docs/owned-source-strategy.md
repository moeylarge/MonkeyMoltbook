# Owned-Source Lead Generation Strategy

## Objective
Build an owned-source hot lead generation system that creates, captures, scores, proves, and operationalizes leads for direct sale to:
- Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) buyers
- Debt Settlement buyers

The system is centered on sources we control:
- landing pages
- city pages
- niche pages
- calculators
- quizzes
- qualification flows
- intake forms
- educational pages
- attribution logic
- scoring rules
- review queues
- buyer-ready exports

## Core business rule
Owned-source leads are the highest-priority asset.

That means the system must maximize:
1. self-submitted inbound leads from our pages
2. clean attribution and consent records
3. qualification depth
4. hot-lead explanations
5. buyer-ready packaging

Secondary priority:
- lawful MCA business prospecting from public business sources that we collect and normalize ourselves

## Lead engines

### Engine 1 — Owned Inbound Lead Engine
Purpose:
Generate self-submitted leads from pages and funnels we own.

Primary outputs:
- MCA inbound leads
- Debt inbound leads

Why it matters:
- strongest proof of intent
- strongest resale value
- clearest source ownership
- easiest to score as hot

### Engine 2 — Owned MCA Prospecting / Public-Data Engine
Purpose:
Generate business-funding prospects from lawful public business data collected by our own system.

Primary outputs:
- MCA business prospect records
- enriched review-ready prospects
- scored buyer-ready MCA prospect leads

Why it matters:
- supplements inbound inventory
- creates controllable prospect pipeline
- expands business coverage by niche and geography

## System priorities
1. MCA owned inbound
2. Debt owned inbound
3. MCA public-data prospecting
4. dashboard, proof, and export readiness

## Source buckets
Every lead must belong to one source bucket:
- inbound_landing_page
- inbound_quiz
- inbound_calculator
- inbound_form
- inbound_consultation
- inbound_city_page
- inbound_niche_page
- mca_public_directory
- mca_public_business_site
- mca_public_social_profile
- mca_public_linkedin_company
- mca_public_listing

## Owned inbound page architecture
Each page must do one job:
- attract one specific intent
- explain one problem clearly
- present one offer clearly
- push to one primary CTA
- capture attribution
- feed one qualification path

Page families:
- main vertical pages
- niche pages
- geo pages
- qualification pages
- comparison pages
- FAQ pages
- calculator pages
- quiz pages
- consultation pages

## Funnel doctrine
Every owned page must route to one of:
- short qualification form
- multi-step quiz
- calculator + lead capture
- consultation request

No dead-end content pages.
No informational pages without CTA.

## Proof doctrine
A lead is not buyer-ready unless we can prove:
- exact page or source URL
- timestamp
- collected inputs
- attribution trail
- scoring explanation
- temperature reason
- consent record where required

## Vertical split

### MCA
Use both:
- owned inbound engine
- public-data prospecting engine

### Debt Settlement
Use only:
- owned inbound engine

No cold consumer scraping.
No debt-stressed consumer list building.
No hidden or private consumer data acquisition.

## Hot lead standard
A hot lead must include:
- fit signal
- strong contactability
- recent submission or verification
- attribution proof
- qualification depth
- reason code explaining why the lead is hot
