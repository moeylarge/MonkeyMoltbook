# Hot Lead Definitions

## MCA hot lead definition
A hot MCA lead is a business-funding lead that we can attribute, explain, and operationalize because it has:
- a clear owned-source or self-collected source path
- strong contactability
- viable business-fit signals
- recent intent or recent verification
- enough qualification depth for review or sale
- a scoring explanation showing why it is high value

### MCA inbound hot lead
Highest-priority MCA lead type.
A self-submitted business lead from a page we own, with complete enough business/contact data and viable funding-fit signals.

### MCA prospecting hot lead
A lawfully collected public business prospect that has strong legitimacy, contactability, fit, and verification signals after enrichment and dedupe.

## Debt hot lead definition
A hot debt lead is a self-submitted, consented, attributable debt-relief inquiry from a page we own, with complete enough contact data, clear distress or qualification indicators, and a recorded explanation for why it is high intent.

## Non-hot categories
### Warm
Potentially usable, but missing stronger fit, urgency, or completeness.

### Cold
Low-intent, low-fit, or weakly supported.

### Junk
Spam, broken, deceptive, duplicate unresolved, or unusable.
